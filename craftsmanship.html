Precision in Every Component

We believe that a shoe is only as good as the sum of its parts. Each stage of our construction is handled by a specialist master artisan in our Marikina atelier.

Process
Stage 01

Leather Selection

Only the top 5% of full-grain calfskin makes it to our cutting table. We source from world-renowned tanneries that prioritize sustainable, vegetable-tanning processes, ensuring a hide that develops a unique character over time.

  • verified Grade-A European hides
  • eco Sustainable Tanning
Stage 02

The Last

The soul of the shoe. We carve our bespoke lasts from solid maple, shaped to the exact anatomical measurements of the client's foot. It is here where comfort and aesthetic silhouette are perfectly balanced.

"The last is the foundation. If the foundation is flawed, the shoe will never truly belong to the wearer."

Stage 03

The Welt Stitch

Using a technique passed down through generations of Marikina artisans, the hand-welted construction connects the upper, insole, and welt with a single, continuous stitch. This ensures unparalleled durability and allows for the shoe to be resoled for decades.

architecture Explore Construction Methods
Stage 04

The Patina

Each pair of Sapatero shoes is hand-painted, layer by layer. This finishing process creates a depth of color that no machine can replicate—a unique patina that shifts in the light and grows more beautiful with every wear.
