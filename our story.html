The Marikina Legacy

For over a century, the rhythm of hammers against leather has been the heartbeat of Marikina. This is not just an industry; it is a shared ancestry. At Sapatero, we carry the weight of this heritage in every stitch, preserving the secrets passed down through generations of master shoemakers.

Our purpose goes beyond commerce. We are custodians of a fading art form. By marrying traditional Filipino resilience with global standards of luxury, we ensure that the legendary craftsmanship of our home continues to walk the world's most prestigious avenues.
